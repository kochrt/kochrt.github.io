Thank you for testing pîng.

Pîng lets you get information about stuff around you simply by pointing your phone at it. Just point your phone, tap its color, and, if its pîngable, you should get information about it. You can also pîng other nearby people if they have the app.

What to test

Basically I need to know if it works, obviously, in different cases and situations. There is only so much testing I can do by myself, so I just want you to try it out.

Feedback and Suggestions

Please email me if you find bugs or have suggestions for improvement. If you don't tell me I won't know about it!
